Around the world, many parking authorities or services that provide vehicle-parking facilities are striving to improve finding a spot in giant parking spots at a convenient location. Currently, parking lots have not evolved and finding a parking spot in busy areas such as malls, amusement parks, etc. can be a hassle during certain periods of time. As drivers search for a parking space for their vehicle, accidents and traffic congestions take effect. These traffic congestions can result in wasting fuel, time and also causes air pollution.

To provide an effective solution to the problem, a technology driven platform has been implemented. Smart Parking technology enables drivers to find parking space quickly and efficiently. So the basic idea is to implement a sensor based system under each parking spot that recognizes whether or not the spot is taken by another car. All the information is recorded and displayed on the web and mobile applications that shows accurate information to drivers of parking spaces that are currently available or occupied by another vehicle. The improvement with this technological innovation results in less traffic congestions, therefore less chances of collisions. This also saves time as well as fuel.

The user would have to interact with the Smart Parking system through an android smartphone using the mobile app, the desktop app or the web app (from any device with internet access). The mobile, desktop and web application provide an up-to date user friendly interface displaying all available and unavailable spots to save the user’s time
